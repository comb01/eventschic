About Us

I’m Evelyn Garcia, a designer with a passion for creating event designs. As well as organizing and managing weddings, parties and events, we reinvent spaces to get the most out of the setting. Each season, we showcase our most special set designs, in which we use a very definitive style adapted to your ideas and our vision. The magic lies in an eclectic array of carefully selected props that we love placed in a bespoke display just for you! Each project is different to the one before. What is most important is that everything is perfect. Thank you for stopping by the site, my team and I would love to hear from you, so please do not hesitate to call or email us with any questions.
