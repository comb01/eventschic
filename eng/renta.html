Equipment Available

We have a range and variety of equipment and furnishings for events we have available for your event:

Chairs & Chair Covers

Chairs & chair covers tie the room together and extend the theme of your wedding to each and every one of your special guests. Our selection of materials, colours, and appearances are available for DIY rentals or full decorating service. Select your style from our gallery or call to learn more about our full selection.

Backdrops

The backdrop for head table, cake table, or dance floor provides the atmosphere for important moments at your reception like speeches, cutting the cake, and your first dance as husband and wife. Browse our gallery of backdrops to see how these essential wedding décor elements can make you and your guests really stand out.

Tablecloths

Every table at your wedding should look great for you and your guests, so we offer a wide variety of tablecloths to dress them up and make every seat a part of your wedding dream. Check out our selection of table cloths in the gallery.

Accessories

Details & Accessories bring a unique and memorable style to your Event dream. See what we have in our gallery, and give us a call if you have an idea or want to get creative by adding to our growing collection of Wedding Dream accessories.

Centerpieces are the focal point of each and every guest table at your wedding. They’re part sculpture, part conversation starter, and 100% memorable feature of your special day. Each centerpiece is unique, but our gallery has examples of past beautiful arrangements that make tables really pop!

Photobooth

Having an event or wedding coming up? And want to capture the fun at? Our Photo Booth will take your event to the next level of fun!

Table Skirting

No head table is complete without a table skirt. They’re not just about hiding the wedding party’s legs – your table skirt is an important part of the picture around the bride & groom and the people closest to them. Our selection is on display here in our gallery.
